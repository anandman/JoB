Simple Strategy — Jacks or Better

Scan your hand from the top down. Hold the first match you find, discard the rest.

Works for all 4 variants. Costs only ~0.08% vs. perfect play on 9/6.

Key Definitions

High Card
Jack, Queen, King, or Ace
Low Pair
Any pair of 2s through 10s
Outside Straight
Open-ended, can complete at either end (e.g., 7-8-9-10)
Inside Straight
Needs a middle card or one-ended (e.g., A-2-3-4 or 6-7-_-9-10)

Counterintuitive Plays

  • Break a Full House, Flush, or Straight for 4 to a Royal Flush.
  • Break a Straight for 4 to a Straight Flush.
  • Hold 3 to a Royal over 4 to a Flush.
  • Hold a low pair over an outside straight draw.
  • With 3+ unsuited high cards, keep the lowest 2.
